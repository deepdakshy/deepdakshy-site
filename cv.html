// about

Born in Kolkata. Based in Berlin. Working everywhere in between.

I started in photojournalism and graphic design — both equally, and neither felt like enough on its own. I studied cinematography at MET Film School and ended up doing all of it: directing, shooting, producing, designing.

Commercially I run campaigns across European markets. Independently I make films — character-driven, intimate, small crews. The two worlds feed each other.
