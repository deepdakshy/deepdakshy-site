// about.txt

Born in Kolkata. Based in Berlin. Working everywhere in between.

I'm a cinematographer, director and creative producer. I've shot and directed five films, run paid social campaigns across eight European markets, and started out doing both photojournalism and graphic design before either of those felt like enough.

I make films because the camera lets me capture what goes unsaid — a feeling that never found words, a moment that passed before anyone noticed. If one person leaves and calls someone they love, or sits quietly and feels grateful to be alive, that's enough. That's the whole point.
